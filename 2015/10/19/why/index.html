Why?

19 October 2015 on Introduction, About Me

-Why a new blog? I'm writing this blog to document my journey into and through the world of functional programming.

I hope to explore and share my findings, trials, failures and successes in the world of programming, as well as other tidbits that catch my attention.

I am a "born-again" coder, having received formal instruction in my younger years (with Java and C++), but sadly due to how the curriculum was presented, nothing really stuck, and programming became a tedious chore. I still struggle to this day on "grasping" how the object oriented paradigm works in the context of these languages.

Despite this personal setback, I stuck with scripting and tinkering around with languages (mostly python) in a Linux environment for personal use in various tasks (I am far from even "good" at Python, for my uses it was basically a glorified scripting language).

I've recently been bitten by several bugs that have pushed me back towards programming, so I've found reason to dive back into the wide world of coding. It took me quite some time to find all the tools, and especially the right language to dedicate my time to (as this is now a hobby for me), but I feel I've done it. However, that journey is for a future post. I hope to see you there.

--The Perplexed One
